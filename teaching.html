Teaching - Spring 2025

Electromagnetism & Electromagnetism Education I

This course explores the foundational concepts of electric and magnetic fields, Maxwell’s equations, and pedagogical strategies for teaching these topics effectively at the secondary and undergraduate levels.

Mathematical Physics I

This course covers mathematical methods commonly used in theoretical physics, including differential equations, Fourier analysis, and linear algebra with applications to physical systems.

Thermal and Statistical Physics

This course introduces the principles of thermodynamics and statistical mechanics, connecting microscopic models to macroscopic observables such as temperature, entropy, and heat capacity.
